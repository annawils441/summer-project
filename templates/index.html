Hatfield Junior Swimming School

Welcome to the HJSS Homepage!

The Hatfield Junior Swimming school does grades 1-5 lessons for pupils aged 4-11. There are also lane swim sessions and family fun sessions! Navigate to the Timetable section for further details.

Grades and Programmes

There are grades ranging from 1 to 5 for pupils aged from 4 to 11. Pupils will be assessed by a coach when they first start lessons to see which grade they should start at! New swimmers will start from grade 1. We do other programmes for older service users such as Aqua Aerobics for over 60s and SwimDance for 18+. For more information on our programmes, check your emails once registered!
